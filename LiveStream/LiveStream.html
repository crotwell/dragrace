NHRA 4-Wide Nationals Ground Motion Experiment at zMAX Dragway

Can you feel the ground shaking as the dragrace cars zoom by? We are measuring the ground shaking of dragrace cars at the zMAX Dragway with seismic devices! Students and faculty from the University of South Carolina have created four devices to do the job! They are deployed on all four lanes. The ground shaking is measured in "g's" (gees) which is a percent of gravitational acceleration. For reference, 1 g is what it takes to lift up an object off the ground!
